Electronic Piano

December 21, 2021

I built this electronic piano circuit using circuit designs online. As a musician, I was very excited to build it because I wanted to see how electric signals are converted to audio signals.

This circuit uses a 555 timer and plays a C-Major scale. My favorite part of making this circuit was tuning each note. It took a little bit of trial and error for me to realize that the higher the resistance the lower the note. Once I realized that, I used a guitar tuner app on my phone, and it was a lot of trial and error until the note was in tune. I also learned the resistors had to be placed in series NOT in parallel.

Something I wanted to do was include sharps and flats but I ran out of buttons.
