REFLEX TIMER

February 4, 2022

I built this reflex timer circuit using a circuit design out of the Make: Electronics book. The purpose of the circuit is to time your reflexes.

There were two tricky parts to the circuit: 1) debugging the circuit was close to impossible because there were so many wires and 2) I didn’t really understand how the circuit worked so that also made debugging tricky.

To fix the first problem, I actually rebuilt the entire circuit using shorter wires so you could easily see the connections (this has become my standard practice now, making sure to build it as organized as possible). To fix the second problem, I spent hours researching every element in addition to reading the book’s explanation.
